DCA
The full form of DCA is a Diploma in Computer Application. It is a one-year computer-based diploma or course. The course is intended to provide learners with basic, realistic and technological details pertaining to programming tools and applications which are used in our everyday lives.

Mechanic Computer Hardware is a computer hardware mechanical vocational trade.
